Below are the AWS community events I participated in during my internship.

---

### [Event 1 - Prompt Engineering, AI Mindmap & BMAD Method](4.1-event1)

This one had three main parts: how to write better prompts so LLMs give more useful answers, a mindmap on effective AI interaction combined with AWS architecture, and the BMAD method, an open-source framework that uses AI to guide structured software development. I found the prompt engineering part the most practical since I could apply it right away.


### [Event 2 - AWS Vietnam Community Day 2026](4.2-event2)

This was a bigger event with multiple speakers sharing real-world experience on AI and cloud. The talks that stood out to me the most were Build Second Brain (how to provide the right context to AI), CloudFront from edge to origin, and the multi-agent credit scoring system for startups. There were also sessions on the LotusHacks hackathon experience and LLM non-determinism, all pretty interesting.
